Prizes and awards

Poczuj miastozen w Kielcach - w równowadze z mieszkańcami (PL)

Feel the city-zen in Kielce - in balance with the citizens (EN)

1st place in the competition "Webskie Kielce. Datathon Idea Kielce 2022" organized by Kielce City Hall.

category: Kielce - a city space in a new form

This is a team work that I was the leader of. Our work focused on testing the concept of a 15-min city for 3 age groups. We used grid analysis to see which neighbourhoods in the city provide the residents' needs within the 15-min. We showed using a hexagonal gridwhere access to selected services and residents' needs are lacking. We proposed a completeness index as an indicator of quality of life in the hexagonal grid. We suggested other data sources and next actions. The maps were created in ArcGIS Online and ArcGIS Pro Desktop. We carried out the analyses using ArcGIS and QGIS. Processes were automated using graphical models.

Technology: vector data, network analysis, density, automation with graphical model, application of selected indicator

Nowe rowerowe Kielce (PL)

New cycling Kielce (EN)

1st place in the competition "Webskie Kielce. Datathon Idea Kielce 2021" organized by Kielce City Hall.

category: "Let's play at building a better Kielce for the next generations"

This is a team work that I was the leader of. The work focused on checking access to sports facilities around schools by bicycle. The main aim was to present the current cycling situation in the city and to propose indicators to check the development of cycling infrastructure in the city. We proposed other sources of crowdsourcing data and the possibility of combining different data to create other indicators. The maps were created in ArcGIS Online and ArcGIS Pro Desktop. We carried out the analyses using ArcGIS and QGIS.

Technology: vector data, density, application of selected indicator
